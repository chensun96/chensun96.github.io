I am a third year Ph.D student from University of Iowa and being advised by Prof. Rishab Nithyanand. I also had the pleasure of working under the mentorship of Prof. Supreeth Shastri previously. My research interests are primarily focused on online privacy. My research interests centered on online privacy. My work focuses on privacy measurement, identifying privacy issues, and creating tools that simplify and improve compliance with data protection regulations.

Research Projects

  • WebMonetize Insight: Decoding Website Revenue Streams (ongoing)
    • Analyze and categorize the various monetization strategies employed by websites. This involves creating a comprehensive framework to identify and understand the different revenue models, such as affiliate marketing, sponsored content, display advertising, product sales, and membership programs.
  • Tradeoffs in the Design and Implementation of Right to be Forgotten (ongoing)
    • Analyzing the legal requirements of RTBF and implementing RTBF in Elasticsearch to explore the different levels of the compliance tradeoff.
  • Establishing the State of the Art for GDPR enforcement
    • Defined GDPR SOTA to reduce uncertainty in complying with GDPR.
    • Used BeautifulSoup and Selenium to build an information archival system to automate crawling all GDPR related documents.
    • Automated label key characteristics of each document and systematic analyze their enforcement trends.
    • Used React to created a website allows users to filter and download GDPR documents by country, articles, penalty etc.
